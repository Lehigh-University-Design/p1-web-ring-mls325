About Masego

Masego is a multi-talented musician known for blending jazz, R&B, and hip-hop into a smooth, genre-defying sound. A singer, songwriter, producer, and saxophonist, he often refers to his style as "TrapHouseJazz." Masego gained widespread recognition with his hit single "Tadow," showcasing his improvisational skills and unique musical flair. His music is characterized by its soulful melodies, intricate saxophone solos, and a laid-back vibe that reflects his playful yet introspective personality. His live performances are equally captivating, often highlighting his versatility as a one-man band.
